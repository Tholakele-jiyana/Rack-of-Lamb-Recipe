Rack of Lamb


Ingredients


  • Rack of lamb
  • Lamb rub
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh thyme
  • Garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Instructions

Cut your rack of lamb into chops
Season using lamb rub, salt, pepper and thyme
(or any of your preffered spices).
Pour olive oil (or any oil of your choice)
to cover the pan and put on medium heat.
Place the chop in the pan and fry 2 min on each side
Add butter, fresh thyme and garlic cloves
Continue frying the chops on both side until you reach
your preffered level of doneness

Chops are ready to be served
Best served with salad or veggies
